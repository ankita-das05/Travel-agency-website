Gangtok Tour Packages

Gangtok is the capital of the mountainous northern Indian state of Sikkim. Established as a Buddhist pilgrimage site in the 1840s, the city became capital of an independent monarchy after British rule ended, but joined India in 1975. Today, it remains a Tibetan Buddhist center and a base for hikers organizing permits and transport for treks through Sikkimâ€™s Himalayan mountain ranges.

Gangtok

  • Arrival in Gangtok
  • On arrival at NJP Railway Station/Bagdogra Airport, pickup by our representative and transfer to the hotel in Gangtok.
  • Check-in at your hotel and spend a leisurely evening.
  • Explore the beautiful and quaint hill town, dotted with charming monasteries and breathtaking landscapes.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.
  • Gangtok Sightseeing
  • After breakfast, get ready for a day of local sightseeing in Gangtok.
  • Visit Handicraft Centre, followed by a visit to Bakthang Falls.
  • Then proceed to Tashi Viewpoint, Ganesh Tok, Do Drul Chorten, Namgyal Institute of Tibetology, and Flower Show.
  • Next, visit MG Marg where you can try the local tea. You can also shop at the local market here.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.
  • Excursion to Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir
  • After breakfast, start for an excursion to Tsomgo Lake and Baba Mandir which is 55 kms from Gangtok city. Situated at an altitude of 12,400 feet, the splendorous lake is the most visited tourist attraction on your Gangtok itinerary.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.
  • NOTE: If you'd like to visit Natula pass, we can add that in your package with an extra applicable cost of INR 4000 - 4500 per vehicle.
  • Departure
  • After breakfast, check-out from the hotel.
  • Drop at Bagdogra Airport/NJP Railway Station to catch your onward flight/train back home.
